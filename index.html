ABOUT THE GAME

A puzzle built on order and precision.

Screw Puzzle turns a mechanical concept into a clever brain teaser. Each wooden board contains bars secured by screws, and your goal is to release them in the correct sequence. You’ve got to figure out which screws are holding everything together — pull the wrong one and you’re stuck. It’s a clean logic experience that rewards patience and careful observation.

How To Play

Remove the screws
Tap to unscrew and remove them from the wooden board. Each action changes the structure of the puzzle, sometimes opening new possibilities and sometimes blocking them.
Find the correct order
Not all screws can be removed safely at once. You must determine which bars depend on others and choose the correct removal sequence.
Use support tools wisely
When things get tricky, special items can give you a second chance. But timing matters — using them too early might waste your advantage.

FAQ

  • What type of game is Screw Puzzle?
    Screw Puzzle is a logic-based puzzle game focused on removing screws in the correct order. Every level throws a wooden board at you that you've got to take apart in just the right way. It’s designed to challenge planning and observation skills rather than speed. You really need to plan your moves or you'll end up stuck.
  • Is the game difficult?
    The early levels are accessible and introduce the mechanics gradually. As you progress, the puzzles become more intricate and require deeper planning. Difficulty increases steadily rather than suddenly. Players who enjoy logical thinking will appreciate the challenge curve.
  • What are support items used for?
    When you're totally stuck, these support items can bail you out. They give you some wiggle room when nothing seems to work. However, they must be used wisely because each one has value. Proper timing can make the difference between failure and success.
  • Can I play without an internet connection?
    Yes, Screw Puzzle supports offline play. You can solve levels anytime without relying on connectivity. This makes it convenient for travel or situations where internet access is limited. Gameplay remains smooth and uninterrupted.
  • How many levels are available?
    The game offers hundreds of levels across a wide difficulty range. New patterns and board layouts keep the experience fresh. As you advance, challenges become more layered and complex. So you’ll definitely have plenty to keep you busy for a while.
