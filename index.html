ABOUT THE GAME

A puzzle built on order and precision.

Screw Puzzle turns a mechanical concept into a clever brain teaser. Each wooden board contains bars secured by screws, and your goal is to release them in the correct sequence. You’ve got to figure out which screws are holding everything together — pull the wrong one and you’re stuck. It’s a clean logic experience that rewards patience and careful observation.

How To Play

Remove the screws
Tap to unscrew and remove them from the wooden board. Each action changes the structure of the puzzle, sometimes opening new possibilities and sometimes blocking them.
Find the correct order
Not all screws can be removed safely at once. You must determine which bars depend on others and choose the correct removal sequence.
Use support tools wisely
When things get tricky, special items can give you a second chance. But timing matters — using them too early might waste your advantage.

Tools & Items

As levels grow more complex, support items become part of your strategy. They aren’t shortcuts — they’re tactical tools. Use them when logic alone isn’t enough to clear a tight configuration.

Level Progression

Screw Puzzle offers hundreds of levels ranging from beginner-friendly to seriously challenging. Early boards introduce the mechanics, while later ones demand careful calculation and forward planning. The harder it gets, the more coins you'll earn - which is good because you'll need them to unlock tools and enhance your gameplay options.

What Changes Over Time:

  • More complex screw layouts
  • New wooden bar arrangements
  • Tighter logical dependencies
  • Greater need for strategic thinking
